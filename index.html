About ImgTool

ImgTool is a free and easy-to-use online image utility that helps you quickly compress and resize your images without compromising on quality. Whether you're optimizing images for websites, social media, or presentations, ImgTool provides a seamless experience right in your browser.

This tool is perfect for students, developers, content creators, and anyone who wants to optimize images on the go.
